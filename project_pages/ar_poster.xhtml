Augmented Reality Song Poster

The task for this project was to create an augmented reality song poster using Adobe Aero. First the still poster was created using Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Photoshop, then the individual components were imported into Adobe Aero and animated.

Using Adobe Aero, viewers can direct their camera at the assigned 'trigger image' (see below) to activate the augmented reality poster.
