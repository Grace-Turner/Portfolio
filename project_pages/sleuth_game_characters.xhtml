Character Designs

The Detective

The Detective is one of the playable characters in the game. They have the special ability of combining clues to make additional clues for solving the murder case.

The Forensic Scientist

The Forensic Scientist is one of the playable characters in the game. They have the special ability to collect scientific evidence.

The Photographer

The Photographer is one of the playable characters in the game. They have the special ability to photograph visual evidence.

The Police Officer

The Police Officer is one of the playable characters in the game. They have the special ability to defend against the attacker using their gun.

The Psychologist

The Psychologist is one of the playable characters in the game. They have the special ability to find extra information about suspects' relationships and whereabouts.
