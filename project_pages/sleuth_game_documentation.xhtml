Projects

Sleuth Game

This is the work-in-progress for my major project. I am creating the components for a 3D cooperative multiplayer deductive horror computer game designed for PC and laptops. I created a variety of prototypes before deciding on the final plan for a murder mystery set in the 1920s (see documentation below).

Documentation

Environment Design

The mansion that the murder mystery is set in is French-Chateau inspired with several rooms to allow for extended gameplay.

Environment Design

The mansion that the murder mystery is set in is French-Chateau inspired with several rooms to allow for extended gameplay.

Modelling

After finalising the design, I began the modelling process for the environment. I started off by creating blueprints for the front of the building and each floor, using a block as a size reference for the playable characters.

I am currently in the process of blocking out the main building structure in Autodesk Maya

Character Designs

The Detective is one of the playable characters in the game. They have the special ability of combining clues to make additional clues for solving the murder case.

The Forensic Scientist is one of the playable characters in the game. They have the special ability to collect scientific evidence.

The Photographer is one of the playable characters in the game. They have the special ability to photograph visual evidence.

The Police Officer is one of the playable characters in the game. They have the special ability to defend against the attacker using their gun.

The Psychologist is one of the playable characters in the game. They have the special ability to find extra information about suspects' relationships and whereabouts.
