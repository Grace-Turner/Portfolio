High-Poly Gramophone Model

The task for this project was to create a high-poly object model. I chose to model a gramophone and used a variety of reference images for different parts of the model. I blocked and modelled the gramophone in Autodesk Maya, starting with a low poly model and then adding detail. After modelling, I unwrapped the UVs and exported them. I drew a custom texture for the embossing on the metal components by exporting the UVs and drawing a design on them in Adobe Photoshop. I then imported the other UVs and the custom embossing designs into Adobe Substance Painter to apply texturing and created a turntable in Adobe Substance Stager.
