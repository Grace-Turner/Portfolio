High-Poly Witch Environment Models

This was my contribution to a collaborative project. The task was to model and texture a high-poly 3D environment. My teammate and I decided to create a witch hut environment. I was assigned the modelling and texturing of the shelves, fireplace, tree and door, and any associated items e.g. potion bottles. We planned the layout for the environment, then I blocked and modelled my components in Autodesk Maya, UV unwrapped them and textured the UVs in Adobe Substance Painter.
