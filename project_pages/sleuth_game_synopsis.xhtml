Sleuth Game

Synopsis

Sleuth takes place in the 1920s, in the French Chateau of a wealthy family. A murder has taken place in the mansion, and the players (up to five) have to figure out who the culprit is. However, the culprit still lurks around the building, and will attack the investigative team in order to stop them solving the case. Each of the playable characters has their own unique special ability that can be used to help them solve the case or fight off the attacker. The game is a deductive horror/murder mystery that requires the players to cooperate to solve the case.
