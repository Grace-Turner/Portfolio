Flavoured Milk Packaging Design

Strawberry Flavour

Chocolate Flavour

Banana Flavour

The task for this project was to design some kind of food or drink packaging in three different flavour variations for the brand "First Foods". I used pre-existing milk carton images and worked on top of them in Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Photoshop to create designs for flavoured milk in strawberry, chocolate and banana flavours. I used pre-existing images as substitutes for elements such as the barcode, recycling instructions, ingredients list and made in Australia labels.
